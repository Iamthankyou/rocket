## Key Facts & Numbers

### Ancient Origins (9th-13th Century)
- 9th Century CE: Earliest rocket tech in Song Dynasty China
- Gunpowder: potassium nitrate + charcoal + sulfur
- "Fire arrow" (火箭): bamboo tube + gunpowder, attached to arrow
- Battle of Kai-Feng (1232): First documented military rocket use
- Thrust: ~50-100 N | Range: 100-300m | Isp: ~50-100s
- Key texts: *Huolongjing*, *Wujing Zongyao*

### Theoretical Pioneers (1880s-1930s)
- **Konstantin Tsiolkovsky** (1857-1935) — "Father of Cosmonautics"
  - Published rocket equation 1903: Dv = v_e x ln(m0/mf)
  - Proposed LOX/LH2 in 1903, calculated escape velocity 11.2 km/s correctly
  - Deaf from age 10, self-taught
- **Hermann Oberth** (1894-1989)
  - Independently derived Tsiolkovsky equation (1923)
  - Founded VfR (1927), mentored von Braun
- **Robert Goddard** (1882-1945)
  - First liquid-fuel rocket: March 16, 1926 (Auburn, MA)
  - Flight: 2.5s, 12.5m altitude, 56m range
  - Fuel: liquid gasoline + LOX
  - 214 rocket patents; NASA Goddard Center named after him
  - Record altitude: 2.6 km (1935)

### V-2 & WWII (1930s-1945)
- **Wernher von Braun** (1912-1972)
- V-2 specs: 14m tall, 13,000 kg, 68.6 kN thrust, 320 km range, 88 km apogee
- Fuel: Ethanol/water + LOX, Isp ~210s
- First successful launch: Oct 3, 1942
- 1,400+ launched at London/Antwerp (1944-45)
- Innovations: turbopump, guidance system, large-scale manufacturing
- Post-war: Operation Paperclip (US), Soviet capture of engineers

### Space Race (1957-1972)
- **Soviet milestones:** Sputnik 1 (Oct 4, 1957), Gagarin (Apr 12, 1961, 108 min)
- **R-7 Semyorka:** Sergei Korolev, 1,038 kN thrust, first ICBM-to-space vehicle
- **US response:** NASA created Oct 1, 1958; Mercury program
- **Apollo Program:** $280B (2024 USD), 400,000+ workers, 12 humans on Moon
- **Saturn V:** 111m tall, 2,970,000 kg, 7,600 kN first stage, 100% crew safety (post-Apollo 1)

### Modern Era (1972-Present)
- **Space Shuttle** (1972-2011): 135 flights, 24,000 kg to LEO, $450M/flight
- **SpaceX revolution:** Falcon 9 (2010), reusable first stage (Dec 2015)
- Cost/kg to LEO: $65,000 (2010) -> $1,500 (2023)
- Launch frequency: ~30/year (pre-2010) -> 150+/year (2023+)

## Key Concepts
- Nguyen ly phan luc (Reaction principle): action-reaction even in ancient rockets
- Tien hoa nhien lieu (Fuel evolution): black powder -> liquid fuel -> cryogenic
- Cuoc dua chinh tri (Political race): Cold War drove rapid innovation
