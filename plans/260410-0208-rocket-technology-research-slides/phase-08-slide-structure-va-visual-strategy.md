## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
| Element | Color | Hex | Usage |
|---------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary background | Deep space black | #0B0D17 | All slide backgrounds |
| Secondary background | Dark navy | #1B2838 | Content panels, cards |
| Accent 1 | Electric blue | #4FC3F7 | Headers, key numbers, orbit lines |
| Accent 2 | Warm orange | #FF7043 | Thrust/fire, highlights, CTAs |
| Accent 3 | White | #FFFFFF | Body text, labels |
| Accent 4 | Cool gray | #90A4AE | Secondary text, captions |
| Success/Go | Green | #66BB6A | Timeline completions, positive stats |
| Alert | Red-orange | #EF5350 | Warnings, critical numbers |

### Typography
- **Titles:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Roboto, or similar), 36-44pt
- **Subtitles:** Semi-bold, 24-28pt
- **Body text:** Regular, 18-22pt, line height 1.5
- **Data/numbers:** Monospace or tabular figures for alignment
- **Vietnamese diacritics:** Ensure font supports full Vietnamese character set (Roboto recommended)

### Layout Principles
- **16:9 aspect ratio** (widescreen standard)
- **Maximum 6 bullet points per slide** (less is more)
- **One key message per slide** — if two messages, split into two slides
- **Visual-to-text ratio:** 60% visual, 40% text minimum
- **Consistent margins:** 5% padding on all sides
- **Grid system:** 12-column for alignment

### Image & Diagram Style
- **Photos:** High-resolution NASA/SpaceX public domain images
- **Diagrams:** Clean vector style, consistent line weight (2-3px)
- **Infographics:** Flat design, solid colors, no gradients on data
- **Charts:** Bar/column preferred over pie for comparison; use accent colors
- **Icons:** Line icons (Lucide, Feather style), 24-32px
- **Animations:** Minimal — fade-in for reveals, no spinning/bouncing

### Infographic Descriptions

1. **Historical Timeline (Slide 3):** Horizontal bar with era segments, icons per milestone, nation flags
2. **Cost Revolution Chart (Slide 5):** Declining curve from $65k to $1.5k per kg, logarithmic Y-axis
3. **Tsiolkovsky Visualizer (Slide 7):** Mass ratio slider concept showing fuel vs structure vs payload
4. **Isp Comparison Bars (Slide 8):** Horizontal bars, length proportional to Isp, engine icons
5. **Rocket Cutaway (Slide 9):** Full-height with callout lines, percentage labels
6. **Trajectory Arc (Slide 12):** Curved path from pad to orbit with phase labels and altitude markers
7. **Earth-Moon Scale (Slide 13):** To-scale distance with 30 Earth diameters annotation
8. **Apollo Descent Profile (Slide 14):** Altitude vs time chart, 9 km to surface
9. **Orbital Altitude Scale (Slide 16):** Concentric rings from Earth surface to lunar distance
10. **Mission Timeline (Slide 17):** 68-year horizontal bar with branching events
11. **Propulsion Roadmap (Slide 19):** Technology maturity chart, current -> 2050 projection
12. **Mars Colony Progression (Slide 20):** 3-panel concept art, 2030 -> 2050 -> 2100

### Presentation Tips
- **Duration:** 30-45 minutes for full presentation, 15-20 for condensed
- **Pacing:** ~2 minutes per slide average; linger on Apollo (3-4 min), quick on data slides
- **Interaction:** Pause after Slide 7 (Tsiolkovsky) for questions — physics is hardest section

- **Video clips:** Apollo 11 landing (30s), Falcon 9 landing (15s), Starship test (15s)
